Vision

Mission

To deliver high-quality, holistic education that strengthens both intellect and character, equipping students to thrive in a changing world.

Our Activities & Extra-curricular

We believe in developing the whole child through diverse, enriching activities that build skills, teamwork, creativity, and leadership.

Sports

Football, basketball, volleyball, athletics promoting fitness, discipline and team spirit.

Debate & Public Speaking

Developing confidence, critical thinking, research skills and eloquent expression.

Music & Performing Arts

Choir, drama, dance encouraging creativity, cultural appreciation and self-expression.

Science & Technology Club

Experiments, robotics, coding — sparking curiosity and innovation.

Celebrating Ugandan heritage alongside global diversity and inclusion.

Community Service

Outreach programs that teach empathy, responsibility and the value of giving back.

Admission Requirements

We welcome new students and families. Please prepare the documents below and contact us for current application forms and deadlines.

  • Completed application form (obtainable from school office or on request)
  • Previous school reports / academic transcripts
  • Birth certificate or passport copy
  • Entrance assessment and/or interview (arranged after application submission)
  • Health / medical records and immunization certificate
  • Two recent passport-size photographs of the student
  • Payment of non-refundable application / registration fee
